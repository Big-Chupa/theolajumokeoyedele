Olajumoke Oyedele

Olajumoke Oyedele is a nurturer commissioned by the Lord to raise, rebuild, restore, and realign destinies. She is a pastor, transformative coach, and founder of Becoming Circle, a platform dedicated to inspiring, transforming, and equipping women to step into their God-ordained purpose. She is also the convener of Blazing Sisters and Blazing Wives, interdenominational fellowships focused on drawing women closer to Christ. Olajumoke is the visionary behind the annual Visionary Women Conference, a gathering that brings women into clarity, direction and strategic alignment for the new year. She is also the founder of the FOAM (Fausat Omolola Awero Memorial) Foundation, which has impacted over 5,000 women and children through maternal and child health initiatives. She is a fellow of the Carrington Youth Fellowship Initiative, associated with the United States Consulate General. She continues to live out her calling - nurturing lives, restoring hope and raising women into the fullness of their God-given identity and purpose.

What We Offer

Work With Me Everything I do is built on one truth — transformation begins when you decide to become. Through my faith-driven and practical approach, I walk with individuals and groups ready to grow, gain clarity, and step boldly into their God-given purpose.
Here’s how you can work with me:
1. Personal & Group Coaching
Join me through my signature coaching platform, Becoming Circle, where I help you discover who you are, break limiting patterns, and align your life with purpose. Whether it’s one-on-one or in group programs, each session is designed to bring clarity, confidence, and transformation.
2. Mentorship & Faith Programs
Be part of my Blazing Sisters and Blazing Wives communities — safe spaces where women are equipped spiritually, emotionally, and mentally to live fulfilled and impactful lives.
3. Speaking Engagements & Trainings
Invite me to speak at your conference, retreat, or leadership event. My sessions are practical, spirit-led, and deeply engaging — covering topics like faith, identity, emotional intelligence, leadership, and purposeful living.
4. Partnerships & Projects
Collaborate with me through the FOAM Foundation to make real impact in maternal and child health initiatives across communities.
Ready to Begin Your Journey? Let’s walk together toward the version of you that God designed from the beginning.
